# Vrinda-Store-Annual-Report
Excel-based annual sales analysis dashboard for Vrinda Store to uncover customer behavior, sales trends, and channel performance.

##  Project Overview
The **Vrinda Store Annual Sales Dashboard** is an interactive **Excel-based data analysis project** created to analyze sales performance for the year **2022**.  
The goal of this project is to help Vrinda Store **understand customer behavior, sales trends, and channel performance** in order to drive higher sales growth in **2023** using **data-driven decisions**.

---

##  Tech Stack
This project was built using the following tools and techniques:
-  **Microsoft Excel** – Data analysis and dashboard creation  
-  **Excel Dataset** – Raw sales and customer data  
-  **Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts** – Data summarization and analysis  
-  **Slicers** – Interactive filtering  
-  **Data Visualization** – Charts for insights and storytelling  

---

##  Data Source
**Source:** Sales data of Vrinda Store for the year 2022.

The dataset includes:
- Order details (sales amount, order count)  
- Customer demographics (age, gender, state)  
- Sales channels (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.)  
- Product categories  

---

##  Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem  
Vrinda Store had raw sales data but lacked **clear insights** into:
- Who their main customers are  
- Which states and channels generate the most sales  
- Which age group and gender contribute the most orders  

This made it difficult to plan marketing and sales strategies for the next year.

---

### • Goal  
To build an **easy-to-understand annual sales dashboard** that:
- Compares sales and orders  
- Identifies top-performing months, states, and channels  
- Analyzes customer behavior by age and gender  
- Supports better business planning for 2023  

---

### • Walkthrough of Key Analysis  
- **Sales vs Orders Chart:** Compares total sales and total orders in a single view  
- **Monthly Sales Analysis:** Identifies the month with the highest sales and orders  
- **Gender-wise Analysis:** Shows purchasing behavior of men vs women  
- **Top 10 States:** Highlights states contributing the most to total sales  
- **Age & Gender Analysis:** Shows relationship between age group and gender based on orders  
- **Channel Analysis:** Identifies channels contributing maximum sales  
- **Category Analysis:** Finds the highest selling product category  
- **Interactive Slicers:** Filter data by category, channel, and time  

---

### • Business Insights
-  **Women customers contribute 65% of total purchases**, making them the primary buyer segment  
-  **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh** are the top 3 sales-contributing states  
-  **Adult age group (30–49 years)** contributes nearly **50% of total orders**  
-  **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra** are the highest contributing sales channels  

---

### • Business Impact & Recommendations
-  Focus marketing campaigns on **women customers aged 30–49 years**  
-  Target high-performing states with regional promotions  
-  Strengthen partnerships and offers on **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra**  
-  Optimize inventory for top-selling product categories  
-  Use customer insights for personalized marketing strategies
